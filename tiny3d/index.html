Behind the scenes of Find Tiny's Family

📖 Story

Tiny the Turtle went on an adventure and the whole family scattered across the neighbourhood, turtle beach, and beyond. The a-REA gang — Aqeel, Oli, Hamid, Alexandru, Harry V, Aadil, Sabira, Zubair (Zuhaib 🙃), and Mirabelle — are all out there too. Help Tiny find everyone!

🎵 Music

All original songs by Aqeel for the a-REA project. The game's vibe is built around the a-REA universe — lore, memes (9+10=21 👀), and inside jokes baked into every corner.

🐢 Tiny

Tiny is the heart of the game. Find Tiny last — in the classroom hideout on Map 3. Tiny is golden and always glowing. The halo is real. 🌟

🔑 Secret Codes

You already know one to be here. There's also a Supporter code earned by finding the space orb after collecting everyone. The dev secret: 212121 is the a-REA secret code itself. 😉

🚀 Space

Fly high on Turtle Beach to blast into space. Or find the watchtower / rocket if you can't fly! The secret orb in space grants Supporter status to those who've found the whole family.

💌 Contact

Enjoyed the game? Feature ideas? Bugs? Hit us up at dacoolkid325@gmail.com — or use the Feature Request button in-game!
